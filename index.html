Chameleon Hearing Protection

Smart, adaptive hearing protection

Designed for industrial environments, Chameleon automatically adapts to the environment. When the environment is quiet, Chameleon is acoustically transparent, allowing you to hear as if you're not wearing hearing protection. The moment the environment becomes loud, Chameleon closes in an instant to protect your hearing until the environment is safe again.
